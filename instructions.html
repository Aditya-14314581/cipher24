Instructions

Solving the Challenges

  • There are 15 challenges. You must complete the current challenge to move to the next challenge.
  • There are a variety of challenges available and finding answers using hints provided in the form of images or audio.
  • Each challenge includes a description of the question as well as a puzzle (image or a link which will direct you to the puzzle). Solve the puzzle and enter your answer in the box.
  • If you're using a computer or laptop, and the images appear to be too small. To get a clear view of the image, zoom the entire screen.
  • Your progress is saved in real-time, so you can close the browser window at any time and revisit the portal later. Do not clear cookies or cache and use the same device and browser if you wish to do so.
  • If you refresh the page, press on continue button to start from where you left off.
  • If you have any query, you can click on 'Submit Query' button below the challenges portal and send us your queries.
  • There's no need to submit anything. As you fill complete the challenges, your answers will be automatically recorded.
  • For the best experience, view the site on desktop.
  1. This is an individual participation competition.
  2. Students from any engineering background can participate in this competition.
  3. Only one answer is correct for each challenge. Instructions, if any, will be mentioned in the challenge itself.
  4. We will also be posting hints/corrections and other general queries on the portal itself.
  5. Timestamp of the submission will be considered in case of a tie.
  6. Any form of plagiarism using social media or other means will lead to immediate disqualification.
  7. The final decision-making authority lies with Team AZeotropy, IIT Bombay.

Leaderboard

  1. A leaderboard will be maintained with the scores of all participating students for every hour.
  2. The position on the leader board is not indicative of the final results.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Prizes worth Rs.15,000 will be awarded to the top winners based on the total number of points scored. The submission timestamp will be used in case of a tie in terms of final points.
